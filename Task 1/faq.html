FAQs

What are the steps to be followed for Fund Transfer?

Step 1: Click on Send Money Button.

Step 2: A prompt will be displayed, please Enter the Email ID of the person you want to transfer money.

Step 3: Enter the Amount to be tranferred.

Step 4: Hit Enter to confirm transaction.

About Atlass Bank?

Atlas Bank Limited is an Indian private sector bank headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka. It sells financial services to large and mid-size companies, SMEs and retail businesses. As of 30 June 2021, 30.81% shares are owned by the promoters and the promoter group.

What is the transaction limit?

The Transaction Default Limit is €. 8000. You cannot exceed that amount.

Is this facility available for NRI customers?

Yes, NRI customers can transfer funds from their NRI account to a resident account.
